A Home, Not Just a Facility

Fortuna Family Home is a licensed adult residential care home where residents receive personalized care in a small, family-style environment.

We believe in dignity, comfort, and connection. Our staff treats every resident like family—with respect, patience, and genuine care. Whether your loved one needs assistance with daily activities, medication management, or simply a safe and nurturing place to call home, we’re here.
